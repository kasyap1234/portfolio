Discover practical ways to reduce waste, save energy, and live more sustainably.

Reduce Single-Use Plastics

Refuse single-use plastics like straws, bags, and water bottles. Opt for reusable alternatives instead.

Conserve Water

Take shorter showers, fix leaks promptly, and install low-flow fixtures to save this precious resource.

Use Energy-Efficient Appliances

Replace traditional incandescent bulbs with LED bulbs and use energy-efficient appliances to reduce your carbon footprint.

Compost Food Waste

Turn food scraps into nutrient-rich soil for your garden by composting. This reduces landfill waste and creates fertilizer.

Cycle or Use Public Transport

Leave your car behind and opt for cycling, walking, or using public transport for short trips.
